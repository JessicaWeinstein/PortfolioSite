Graphic & Web Designer

Hi, I'm Jessica! I have a passion for the art of visual communication, technology and personal development. I graduated from Drexel University in 2014 with a BS in Graphic Design but have become more interested in web development throughout the years. I am currently working as a Graphic Designer/Web Specialist at Urban Engineers, designing digital and print marketing materials while managing the company's website.

I recently received my web design certificate from The University of the Arts continuing education program. I've always been interested in conquering the challenge of web development and improving my coding skills. Experimenting with HTML, CSS, and JS, I've come to love the process of making my designs live. After much practice and hard work, I now enjoy the endless possibilities that this path offers and relize that writing code is a creative process in itself. Having the skills to design and code gives me the ability to create beautifully designed websites that come to life.
