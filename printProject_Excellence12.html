Excellence Magazine 12

Excellence is Urban Engineers's magazine which showcases featured projects, employees and company culture. While working with a team of copywriters and designers, I created graphics, illustrations photography, lettering and layout design. I was also involved in our in house print production process.

Issue 12 explored the theme of Rebuild/Restore. It took us in many different directions. From historic bridges to a multimillion-dollar fountain display, and from abandoned infrastructure to a mobile cabin, we dug into a variety of topics. While designing the cover, I wanted each article to be represented within the graphic.
