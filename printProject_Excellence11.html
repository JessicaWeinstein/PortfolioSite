Excellence Magazine 11

Excellence is Urban Engineers's magazine which showcases featured projects, employees and company culture. While working with a team of copywriters and designers, I created graphics, illustrations photography, lettering and layout design. I was also involved in our in house print production process.

Issue 11 focused on the theme "New Frontiers" in order to show case Urban Engineer's new office locations in California and Texas. We wanted it known that Urban is excited to explore work in new territories.
