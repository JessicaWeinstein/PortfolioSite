Excellence Magazine 13

Excellence is Urban Engineers's magazine which showcases featured projects, employees and company culture. While working with a team of copywriters, I created graphics, illustrations photography, lettering and layout design. I also printed each issue with our in house print production process.

In Issue 13, we explored the theme of Legacy. Most engineering projects are completed with the goal of creating an end product that will have a long lifespan. Many project Urban Engineers works on already has a legacy of their own, and their work contributes to enhance and preserve it. This issue provides insight into projects like these and more.
