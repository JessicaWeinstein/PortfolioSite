#UR60N Campaign

Planned and designed social media posts and digital banners for Urban Engineer's #UR60N campaign. The campaign was a year long initiative to celebrate the company's 60th anniversary. As a member of the marketing team, we planned monthly themes to promote Urban's atcheivements as a company. Some monthly themes included diversity, internships, training/education and workplace culture at Urban. We had a total of 12 themes that we promoted throughout the year all using the hashtag #UR60N.
